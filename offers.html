Offers

If you are ready to immerse yourself into new adventures, explore sandy beaches, go hiking, cycling, kayaking, or simply sun bathing on our sun deck, then check our offers. We update them regularly. We are on the island of "Dugi otok" near Zadar, in a little place called Dragove. If you crave serenity, but also need some action, we offer both ! We are situated in the most desirable area, between the two of the most beautiful beaches on the island; Veli zal (2.5 km) and Saharun (9 km). The nearest beach is 1 km away from the property, but the view from the top is always much better then the view from the bay, our panoramic sea view is unbeatable! Considering those two beaches are the ones that everybody visits on a daily basis, being in between is very advantageous. Dragove is a very quiet place and the first grocery shops and restaurants are in the next village, some 5 km away, but that is the part of its charm and it is a perfect getaway from hustle and bustle of cities!
